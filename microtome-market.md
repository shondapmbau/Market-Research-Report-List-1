<p><strong>The Microtome Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sliding</li><li>Rotary</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microtome market is primarily categorized into sliding and rotary types. Sliding microtomes utilize a straight blade that moves back and forth to slice specimens, ideal for embedding materials like paraffin. They offer precision for thick sections. Rotary microtomes, on the other hand, employ a rotating mechanism, allowing for smoother and more consistent sectioning for both soft and hard tissues. Both types are essential in histology and pathology for preparing samples for microscopic examination.</p></p>
