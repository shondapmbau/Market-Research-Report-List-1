<p><strong>The Composite Molding Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Open Molding"</li><li>"Closed Molding"</li><li>"Cast Polymer Molding"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Composite Molding Equipment Market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Open Molding** involves processes where the mold is open to the atmosphere, allowing for the application of resin, often using techniques like hand lay-up or spray-up.</p><p>2. **Closed Molding** utilizes two molds that enclose the material, increasing efficiency and reducing emissions, typically through processes like vacuum infusion or resin transfer molding.</p><p>3. **Cast Polymer Molding** combines resins with fillers to create materials like solid surfaces, offering high aesthetics and durability for various applications.</p></p>
