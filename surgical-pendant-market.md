<p><p>The surgical pendant market is categorized into two primary types: electrical and mechanical. Electrical surgical pendants are powered systems that provide integrated medical gas, electricity, and data connections, enhancing the efficiency of surgical workflows. They often feature advanced functionalities, such as adjustable arms and power supply management. Mechanical surgical pendants, on the other hand, rely on manual operation and are typically simpler in design, offering basic support for equipment without the complexities of electrical integration. Both types play crucial roles in operating rooms.</p></p>
