<p><ul><li>Low Frequency</li><li>Medium Frequency</li><li>High Frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF transformers are categorized based on frequency to serve different applications. Low-frequency transformers operate in the kilohertz range, suitable for power amplification and RF transmission; they emphasize efficiency and thermal management. Medium-frequency transformers, typically in the megahertz range, support communication systems and broadcasting, balancing performance and size. High-frequency transformers, operating in the gigahertz range, cater to advanced technologies like RF identification, Wi-Fi, and telecommunications, focusing on compactness, signal integrity, and minimal loss for enhanced data transmission.</p></p>
