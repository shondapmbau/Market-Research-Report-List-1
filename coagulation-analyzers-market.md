<p><p>The coagulation analyzers market encompasses various testing types that assess blood coagulation. Prothrombin Time Testing measures the time it takes for blood to clot, while Fibrinogen Testing evaluates the level of fibrinogen essential for clot formation. Activated Clotting Time Testing assesses the intrinsic pathway of coagulation. D-Dimer Testing detects fibrin degradation products indicating clotting disorders. Platelet Function Tests gauge platelet activity, and Anti-Factor Xa Tests monitor anticoagulation therapy. Heparin & Protamine Dose Response Tests evaluate treatments, alongside other specialized coagulation assessments.</p></p>
