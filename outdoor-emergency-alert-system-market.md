<p><p>The Outdoor Emergency Alert System market comprises primarily two types: Omni Directional and Rotating Directional. Omni Directional systems emit alerts in all directions simultaneously, ensuring a broad coverage area and reaching individuals from any angle. Conversely, Rotating Directional systems can pivot to direct sound or light signals toward specific areas, providing targeted alerts that focus on particular zones. Both types are crucial in enhancing public safety by delivering timely warnings in emergencies, each serving unique operational needs in alert dissemination.</p></p>
