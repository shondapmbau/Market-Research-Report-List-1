<p><strong>The Exhaust Gas Temperature Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PTC Sensor</li><li>NTC Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The exhaust gas temperature sensor market primarily includes PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) and NTC (Negative Temperature Coefficient) sensors. PTC sensors increase resistance with temperature rises, making them suitable for high-temperature applications, while NTC sensors decrease resistance as temperature rises, allowing for faster response times. Both types are crucial for monitoring exhaust temperatures in automotive and industrial applications, ensuring optimal engine performance, reducing emissions, and enhancing fuel efficiency. The choice between PTC and NTC depends on specific operational requirements and environmental conditions.</p></p>
