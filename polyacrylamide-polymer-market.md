<p><p>The polyacrylamide polymer market is primarily categorized into four types: Non-Ionic Polyacrylamide (PAMN), Anionic Polyacrylamide (APAM), Cationic Polyacrylamide (CPAM), and Others. PAMN is utilized for applications requiring neutral charge, while APAM is effective in wastewater treatment and soil conditioning due to its negative charge. CPAM, with its positive charge, is widely used in food processing and sludge dewatering. The "Others" category encompasses specialty polymers used in niche applications across various industries, including mining and textiles.</p></p>
