<p><p>The food grade sodium alginate market is primarily categorized into two types: powder and granular. Sodium alginate powder is a fine, free-flowing substance that easily dissolves in water, making it ideal for use in thickening, gelling, and stabilizing various food products. In contrast, granular sodium alginate consists of larger particles that may offer different textural properties and dissolution rates, suitable for specific applications like food encapsulation or innovative culinary techniques. Both forms enhance food quality and versatility.</p></p>
