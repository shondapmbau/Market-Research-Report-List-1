<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Steering Wheel ECUs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>REPS</li><li>CEPS</li><li>PEPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Steering Wheel ECU market includes REPS (Rack-mounted Electric Power Steering), CEPS (Column-mounted Electric Power Steering), and PEPS (Pinion-mounted Electric Power Steering). REPS integrates the motor on the steering rack, offering high torque for larger vehicles. CEPS places the motor on the steering column, ideal for compact cars due to its lightweight design. PEPS positions the motor on the pinion, balancing performance and space efficiency, suitable for mid-sized vehicles. Each type caters to specific vehicle needs, enhancing steering precision, efficiency, and driver comfort.</p></p>
