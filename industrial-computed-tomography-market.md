<p><ul><li>High Energy Industrial CT</li><li>Low Energy Industrial CT</li><li>Mini-Focus Industrial CT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Computed Tomography (CT) market comprises three primary types: High Energy Industrial CT, Low Energy Industrial CT, and Mini-Focus Industrial CT. High Energy Industrial CT is used for dense materials and complex geometries, providing high-resolution images for large components. Low Energy Industrial CT targets softer materials, optimizing lower radiation doses for lighter objects. Mini-Focus Industrial CT offers compact systems for high-resolution imaging of small parts, balancing detail and size, making it ideal for precision applications in various industries.</p></p>
