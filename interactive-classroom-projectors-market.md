DLP projectors utilize micro-mirrors for brighter images and better contrast, making them suitable for well-lit environments. LCD projectors provide vibrant colors and sharper images, ideal for detailed presentations. Other types include LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon) and LED projectors, which cater to specific needs and preferences, enhancing the interactive learning experience in classrooms.</p></p>
