<p><strong>The Gas Chromatography Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas-solid Chromatography (GSC)</li><li>Gas-liquid Chromatography (GLC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gas chromatography systems are divided into two primary types: gas-solid chromatography (GSC) and gas-liquid chromatography (GLC). GSC involves the interaction of gaseous samples with a solid stationary phase, predominantly used for separating gases and volatile compounds. In contrast, GLC utilizes a liquid stationary phase to separate components based on their volatility, making it ideal for analyzing complex mixtures in various industries. Both techniques play critical roles in chemical analysis, quality control, and environmental testing applications.</p></p>
