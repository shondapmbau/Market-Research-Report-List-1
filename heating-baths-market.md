<p><ul><li>Water</li><li>Silicone Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heating baths market encompasses two primary types: water and silicone oil. Water baths use water as a medium for even heat distribution, suitable for general laboratory applications and experiments requiring precise temperature control. In contrast, silicone oil baths provide a higher thermal stability and are ideal for applications that demand elevated temperatures without risk of evaporation or flammability. Both types serve diverse industries, including pharmaceuticals, chemical research, and material testing, each offering distinct advantages based on the specific heating needs.</p></p>
