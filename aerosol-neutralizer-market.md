<p><ul><li>Radioactive Neutralizer</li><li>Nonradioactive Neutralizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerosol neutralizer market is classified into two main types: radioactive and nonradioactive neutralizers. Radioactive neutralizers use ionizing radiation to eliminate airborne particles, effectively reducing contamination and enhancing air quality. They are often employed in specialized settings like laboratories and hospitals. Nonradioactive neutralizers, on the other hand, utilize chemical or physical mechanisms to neutralize aerosols without radiation, making them suitable for a broader range of applications, including industrial and residential environments, by focusing on safety and ease of use.</p></p>
