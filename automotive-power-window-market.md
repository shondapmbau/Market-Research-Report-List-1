<p><p>The automotive power window market includes two main types: Cable Type and Gear-Drive Type. Cable Type systems use a flexible cable mechanism to transfer motor power to the window regulator, offering smooth operation and adaptability to various vehicle designs. Gear-Drive Type systems employ a gear mechanism for power transmission, providing durability and precise control, often used in heavier or larger windows. Both types enhance convenience and safety, with their adoption depending on vehicle requirements and design preferences.</p></p>
