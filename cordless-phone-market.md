<p><ul><li>Analog</li><li>DECT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cordless phone market primarily consists of two types: Analog and DECT. Analog cordless phones use traditional radio frequency technology for communication, offering basic features and reliability at lower costs, but may experience interference. DECT (Digital Enhanced Cordless Telecommunications) phones, on the other hand, provide superior sound quality, extended range, and enhanced security due to their digital encryption. DECT technology is favored for residential and business use, as it supports multiple handsets and advanced features, making it a more versatile option.</p></p>
