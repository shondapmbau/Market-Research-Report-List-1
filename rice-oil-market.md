<p><strong>The Rice Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extraction</li><li>Squeezing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rice oil market is primarily divided into two main types based on extraction methods: solvent extraction and cold pressing (squeezing). Solvent extraction utilizes chemicals to extract oil from rice bran, resulting in higher yields and refined products, making it suitable for large-scale production. In contrast, cold pressing, or squeezing, involves mechanically pressing rice bran at low temperatures to retain flavor and nutrients, appealing to health-conscious consumers and niche markets. Both methods cater to diverse consumer preferences and market demands.</p></p>
