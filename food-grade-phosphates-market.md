<p><p>The food-grade phosphates market is segmented into three main types: orthophosphate, polyphosphate, and metaphosphate. Orthophosphates are the most basic form, commonly used as acidity regulators and texture enhancers in food products. Polyphosphates consist of longer chains and are utilized for moisture retention and emulsification, often found in processed meats and dairy. Metaphosphates, characterized by their unique crystalline structure, serve as stability agents and are used in specialty applications like canned goods and food preservatives, enhancing shelf life and quality.</p></p>
