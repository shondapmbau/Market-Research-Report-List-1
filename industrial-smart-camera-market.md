<p><strong>The Industrial Smart Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Scan Camera</li><li>Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Smart Camera market is primarily categorized into Area Scan and Line Scan Cameras. Area Scan Cameras capture images in a rectangular frame, making them suitable for capturing scenes where objects are stationary or randomly positioned, ideal for quality control and inspection. In contrast, Line Scan Cameras capture images one line at a time, making them ideal for high-speed applications like web inspection and continuous materials processing. Both types enhance automation, ensuring precision and efficiency in industrial environments.</p></p>
