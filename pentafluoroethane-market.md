However, stringent environmental regulations and the shift toward low-GWP alternatives, such as hydrofluoroolefins (HFOs), are expected to challenge market growth.</p><p>Recent trends in the Pentafluoroethane Market include the development of eco-friendly refrigerant blends and the increasing use of R-125 in fire suppression systems.
