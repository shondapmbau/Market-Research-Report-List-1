<p><p>The high-end action camera market primarily features two types: box-type cameras and bullet-type cameras. Box-type cameras, characterized by their rectangular shape, offer robust features like advanced stabilization, high resolution, and interchangeable lenses, making them ideal for dynamic sports and professional use. Conversely, bullet-type cameras are more compact and streamlined, often designed for mounting on helmets or bikes, providing ease of use and portability. Both types cater to adventure enthusiasts and content creators seeking durability and high-quality footage in various environments.</p></p>
