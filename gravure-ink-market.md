<p><ul><li>Water Type Gravure Ink</li><li>Alcoholic Type Gravure Ink</li><li>Benzene Type Gravure Ink</li><li>Petrol Type Gravure Ink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gravure ink market is categorized into various types based on their solvent properties. Water-type gravure inks utilize water as a solvent, offering eco-friendly benefits and reduced VOC emissions. Alcoholic-type gravure inks use alcohol solvents, providing faster drying times and better adhesion on non-porous substrates. Benzene-type gravure inks, though less common due to health concerns, deliver high-performance results. Petrol-type gravure inks, using petroleum solvents, are known for their excellent color reproduction but face regulatory challenges due to environmental impact.</p></p>
