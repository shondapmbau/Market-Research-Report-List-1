<p><p>The Thoracic Aortic Covered Stents market is segmented into two primary types: bifurcated and straight tube stents. Bifurcated stents are designed to treat lesions at the branching points of the aorta, facilitating blood flow into both the descending and ascending branches. Straight tube stents, on the other hand, are used for more straightforward lesions along the thoracic aorta, providing a continuous support structure. Both types aim to enhance vascular repair and restore optimal blood flow in thoracic aortic conditions.</p></p>
