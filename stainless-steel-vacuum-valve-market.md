<p><p>The stainless steel vacuum valve market is categorized into manual and pneumatic types. Manual valves require human operation to control the flow of substances, offering simplicity and reliability for low-demand applications. In contrast, pneumatic valves utilize compressed air for automated operation, enabling faster and more precise control, ideal for high-volume or complex processes. Both types are crucial in industries such as food processing, pharmaceuticals, and manufacturing, ensuring efficient vacuum system performance while maintaining durability and corrosion resistance.</p></p>
