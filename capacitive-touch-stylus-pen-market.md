<p><strong>The Capacitive Touch Stylus Pen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Capacitive Touch Stylus Pen</li><li>Passive Capacitive Touch Stylus Pen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The capacitive touch stylus pen market comprises two main types: active and passive capacitive touch stylus pens. Active capacitive stylus pens feature built-in electronics, enabling pressure sensitivity and advanced functionality like palm rejection and programmable buttons, making them ideal for professional applications such as graphic design and note-taking. Conversely, passive capacitive touch stylus pens work by mimicking a finger's touch and do not have electronic components, offering a simpler and more affordable option suited for casual use on touchscreen devices.</p></p>
