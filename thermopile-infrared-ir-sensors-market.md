<p><p>The Thermopile Infrared (IR) Sensors market is divided into two main types: Through Hole Thermopile Infrared Sensors and SMD (Surface Mount Device) Thermopile Infrared Sensors. Through Hole sensors are designed for traditional PCB mounting, providing robust mechanical support and reliability in various applications. In contrast, SMD sensors are compact and suitable for high-density circuit boards, enabling automation in manufacturing and enhancing thermal detection capabilities. Both types are crucial for applications in industries like healthcare, automotive, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
