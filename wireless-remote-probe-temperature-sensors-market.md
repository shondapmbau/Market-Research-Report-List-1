Dual-channel sensors can track two points simultaneously, offering improved flexibility for comparing different locations. Multi-channel sensors are designed to monitor multiple temperature points concurrently, making them suitable for complex systems where extensive data collection is essential. Each type caters to varying needs and complexities in temperature monitoring across diverse industries.</p></p>
