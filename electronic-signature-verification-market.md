<p><strong>The Electronic Signature Verification Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Signature Verification</li><li>Dynamic Signature Verification</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Signature Verification market comprises two main types: static and dynamic signature verification. Static signature verification focuses on analyzing a person's handwritten signature from a static image, assessing its features like stroke patterns and pressure. In contrast, dynamic signature verification evaluates the signature during the act of signing, capturing real-time data such as speed, rhythm, and acceleration. Both methods aim to enhance authentication processes, but dynamic verification offers a more detailed and secure analysis by considering behavioral characteristics.</p></p>
