<p><p>The duct inspection and cleaning robot market comprises three main types: round ducts robots, square ducts robots, and universal robots. Round ducts robots are specifically designed to navigate the curved surfaces of circular ductwork, ensuring effective cleaning and inspection. Square ducts robots cater to the angular structure of square vents, providing tailored solutions for such configurations. Universal robots are versatile units that can operate in both round and square ducts, offering flexibility for diverse HVAC systems while maximizing efficiency in maintenance tasks.</p></p>
