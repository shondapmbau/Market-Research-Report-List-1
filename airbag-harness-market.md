<p><p>The airbag harness market is categorized into three main types: H-shaped, E-shaped, and T-shaped designs. The H-shaped harness features two vertical and one horizontal belt, providing balanced protection across the chest and torso. The E-shaped harness offers a more ergonomic fit with additional straps for improved stability during impacts. The T-shaped design focuses on securing the upper body while allowing for greater freedom of movement, making it popular in sports and recreational vehicles. Each type addresses specific safety needs and comfort levels.</p></p>
