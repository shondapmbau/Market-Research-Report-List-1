<p><strong>The Water-washed Kaolin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Strong Magnetic Separation</li><li>General Magnetic Separation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water-washed kaolin market is segmented based on magnetic separation techniques: strong magnetic separation and general magnetic separation. Strong magnetic separation targets ultra-fine impurities using high-intensity magnetic fields, resulting in high-purity kaolin suitable for advanced applications like ceramics and pharmaceuticals. In contrast, general magnetic separation employs moderate magnetic strength to remove ferrous contaminants, producing kaolin for standard applications in paper and rubber. Each method caters to specific industry needs and desired purity levels, influencing demand and pricing in the market.</p></p>
