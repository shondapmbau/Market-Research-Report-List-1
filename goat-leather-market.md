<p><p>The goat leather market comprises various types based on processing stages. Dried goat leather is fully processed, lightweight, and ready for immediate use. Raw leather is untreated, suitable for initial processing. Tanned or crust leather is prepped to prevent decay, featuring a firm texture suitable for crafting. Wet-blue leather is semi-processed with chromium salts, retaining moisture, and allows flexible further processing. Each type serves distinct applications in fashion, upholstery, and accessories, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
