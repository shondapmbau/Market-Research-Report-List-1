<p><p>The RF inductor market for high-frequency circuits features three main types: film type, wire wound type, and multilayer type. Film type inductors are compact and suitable for high-frequency applications due to their minimal parasitic effects. Wire wound inductors provide superior performance at lower frequencies, making them versatile for various applications. Multilayer inductors combine multiple layers of conductive materials to achieve compact designs with excellent inductance and Q factors, ideal for RF applications where space is a premium.</p></p>
