<p><p>Wet-laid glass fiber mats are non-woven materials made from glass fibers that are arranged and bonded together using a wet-laid process. This method involves dispersing glass fibers in a slurry, which is then deposited on a moving screen to form a mat. The mats are cured, resulting in a lightweight, versatile, and strong product widely used in automotive, construction, and aerospace applications. They offer excellent dimensional stability, thermal resistance, and chemical durability.</p><p>The Wet-laid Glass Fiber Mats Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.8% during the forecast period.
