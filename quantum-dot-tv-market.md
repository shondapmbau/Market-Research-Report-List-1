<p><strong>The Quantum Dot TV Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>QLED</li><li>QDEF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Quantum Dot TV market primarily comprises two types: QLED and QDEF. QLED (Quantum Dot Light Emitting Diode) utilizes quantum dots to enhance color and brightness by converting backlighting into vivid colors, offering high color accuracy and brightness. QDEF (Quantum Dot Enhancement Film) employs a film of quantum dots to improve color output for LCD screens, enhancing their performance without the need for a full redesign. Both technologies aim to deliver superior visual experiences in the expanding display market.</p></p>
