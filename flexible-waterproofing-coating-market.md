<p><strong>The Flexible Waterproofing Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-Component</li><li>Two-Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flexible waterproofing coating market is divided into two main types: one-component and two-component systems. One-component coatings are pre-mixed and ready for application, offering ease of use and quick drying. They are ideal for small projects or DIY applications. Conversely, two-component coatings require a separate hardener to be mixed with the base material before application, resulting in superior adhesion, durability, and resistance to harsh conditions. Each type serves distinct needs based on project scale and performance requirements.</p></p>
