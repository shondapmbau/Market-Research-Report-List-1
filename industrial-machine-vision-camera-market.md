<p><strong>The Industrial Machine Vision Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Line Scan Camera</li><li>Area Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Machine Vision Camera market is primarily divided into two types: Line Scan Cameras and Area Scan Cameras. Line Scan Cameras capture one line of pixels at a time, making them ideal for continuous processes like web inspection or conveyor systems. They excel in high-speed applications with long objects. In contrast, Area Scan Cameras capture a complete image in a single shot, suited for static or slower-moving applications such as quality control and object recognition on assembly lines.</p></p>
