<p><p>Digital Construction Management encompasses two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based systems offer flexibility and scalability, allowing users to access tools and data from anywhere, fostering collaboration among project stakeholders. These solutions often require subscription fees and are managed by third-party providers. On-premise solutions, however, are installed locally on company servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring higher upfront costs and ongoing maintenance. Both types aim to improve efficiency and project outcomes in construction management.</p></p>
