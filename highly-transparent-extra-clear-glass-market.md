<p><p>The highly transparent extra clear glass market encompasses various types, notably rolled glass and float glass. Rolled glass is produced by passing molten glass through rollers, creating a smooth, uniform surface ideal for architectural applications. Float glass, on the other hand, is manufactured by floating molten glass on molten tin, resulting in a flawless and transparent sheet with excellent optical clarity. Both types cater to industries requiring high visibility and aesthetic appeal, such as construction, automotive, and interior design.</p></p>
