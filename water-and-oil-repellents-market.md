<p><p>The water and oil repellents market is primarily divided into two types: fluoropolymer and non-fluoropolymer repellents. Fluoropolymer repellents, known for their excellent water and oil resistance, use fluorinated compounds that provide durable long-lasting protection, commonly utilized in textiles and automotive coatings. In contrast, non-fluoropolymer repellents employ alternative chemical technologies, such as silicones or plant-based materials, offering eco-friendly options with less environmental impact. Both types cater to various industries, including textiles, packaging, and construction, optimizing surface performance.</p></p>
