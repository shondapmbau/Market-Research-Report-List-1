<p><strong>The Cryogenic Coolers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gifford-Mcmahon Cryocoolers</li><li>Pulse-Tube Cryocoolers</li><li>Stirling Cryocoolers</li><li>Joule-Thomson Cryocoolers</li><li>Brayton Cryocoolers</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cryogenic coolers market encompasses various types designed for different applications. Gifford-McMahon cryocoolers use a two-stage process with a moving piston for efficient cooling. Pulse-tube cryocoolers operate without moving parts, enhancing reliability. Stirling cryocoolers utilize a reciprocating movement for cooling efficiency. Joule-Thomson cryocoolers employ gas expansion for temperature reduction, while Brayton cryocoolers use a gas turbine cycle for cooling. Additional types may include advanced designs catering to specific industrial or research needs, broadening the market scope.</p></p>
