<p><strong>The Electronics Coolant Liquids Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Phase</li><li>Two-phase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronics coolant liquids market is categorized into single-phase and two-phase cooling solutions. Single-phase coolants, typically liquids, absorb heat and remain in the same state, making them simpler and easier to manage. In contrast, two-phase coolants utilize both liquid and vapor phases, enhancing heat transfer efficiency by harnessing the latent heat of vaporization. This allows for more effective cooling in high-power electronics, leading to improved performance and reliability in various applications, from data centers to consumer electronics.</p></p>
