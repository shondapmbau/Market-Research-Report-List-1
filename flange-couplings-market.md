<p><ul><li>Rigid Flange Couplings</li><li>Flexible Flange Couplings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flange couplings market is categorized into two main types: rigid and flexible flange couplings. Rigid flange couplings provide a solid connection between shafts, ensuring precise alignment and high strength, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, flexible flange couplings can accommodate slight misalignments and absorb shock loads, enhancing durability and reducing wear. They are ideal for dynamic applications where flexibility is essential, enabling smoother operation in various industrial settings, including manufacturing and power transmission systems.</p></p>
