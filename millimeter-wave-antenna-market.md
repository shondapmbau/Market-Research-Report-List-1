<p><p>The millimeter wave antenna market includes two key types: Antenna in Package (AiP) and Antenna on Package (AoP). AiP integrates the antenna directly within the integrated circuit package, optimizing size and performance for applications like 5G and IoT devices. AoP, on the other hand, places the antenna on top of the package, allowing for easier integration and potential heat dissipation. Both types cater to the growing demand for compact and efficient antennas in advanced communication technologies.</p></p>
