<p><strong>The Lugged Knife Gate Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi Lugged</li><li>Fully Lugged</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lugged knife gate valves are classified into semi-lugged and fully lugged types based on their design and installation features. Semi-lugged valves have lugs on the body for secure mounting without requiring flanges, while still allowing for some flexibility in piping systems. Fully lugged valves, on the other hand, incorporate more robust lugs that enable direct mounting between flanges, ensuring a tighter seal and enhanced stability. Both types are essential for controlling the flow of bulk materials in various industrial applications.</p></p>
