<p><strong>The Demister Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wire-mesh Demister</li><li>Baffle-type Demister</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Demister markets comprise various types designed to separate liquid droplets from gas streams. Wire-mesh demisters utilize layers of knitted wire mesh to diffuse gas flow, minimizing droplet carryover. Baffle-type demisters employ a series of baffles to redirect gas, allowing droplets to coalesce and drain. Other types include mechanical and electrostatic demisters, which utilize different mechanisms like gravity or electrical charges for separation. Each type serves specific applications in industries such as oil and gas, chemical processing, and HVAC systems.</p></p>
