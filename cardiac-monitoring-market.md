<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Cardiac Monitoring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrocardiogram (ECG) Devices</li><li>Implantable Loop Recorder (ILRs)</li><li>Cardiac Event Monitors</li><li>Cardiac Output Monitoring (COM) Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiac monitoring market encompasses various technologies for assessing heart health. Electrocardiogram (ECG) devices record electrical activity to detect abnormalities. Implantable Loop Recorders (ILRs) monitor heart rhythms over extended periods, aiding in diagnosing unexplained arrhythmias. Cardiac Event Monitors provide intermittent monitoring, enabling patients to record symptoms as they occur. Cardiac Output Monitoring (COM) devices measure the heart's efficiency in pumping blood, essential for managing various cardiovascular conditions. Together, these types enhance patient care and outcomes in cardiology.</p></p>
