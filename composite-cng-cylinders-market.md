Type III cylinders consist of an aluminum liner wrapped with composite materials, offering strength and lightweight advantages. Type IV cylinders feature a polymer liner fully encased in composite material, providing even lighter weight and enhanced corrosion resistance. Both types are crucial in the growing Compressed Natural Gas market, catering to various applications, including automotive and industrial sectors, emphasizing safety and efficiency.</p></p>
