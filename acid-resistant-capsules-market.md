<p><p>The acid-resistant capsules market primarily features two types: Hypromellose (HPMC) and Hypromellose Phthalate (HPMCP). HPMC capsules are plant-based, offering an alternative to gelatin, while providing effective protection against acidic conditions, making them suitable for various formulations. HPMCP capsules are specifically designed to withstand gastric fluid, ensuring the release of the active ingredient in the intestines. Both types cater to the growing demand for vegetarian and specialized drug delivery systems, enhancing therapeutic efficacy and patient compliance.</p></p>
