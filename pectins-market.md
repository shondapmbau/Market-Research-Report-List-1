<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Pectins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin</li><li>Low Methoxyl (LMC) Pectin</li><li>Amidated Low Methoxyl (LMA) Pectin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pectin market is categorized into three main types: High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin, Low Methoxyl (LM) Pectin, and Amidated Low Methoxyl (LMA) Pectin. HM Pectin requires higher sugar concentrations for gelling and is commonly used in jams and jellies. LM Pectin gels with lower sugar levels and is ideal for low-sugar or sugar-free products. LMA Pectin, modified with amide groups, offers flexibility in gelling conditions and is suitable for a wider range of applications, including dairy and savory products.</p></p>
