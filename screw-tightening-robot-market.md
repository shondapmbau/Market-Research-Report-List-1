<p><p>The screw tightening robot market primarily features Cartesian and SCARA robots, each offering unique advantages. Cartesian robots utilize a linear axis configuration for precise and straightforward movement, ideal for repetitive screw tightening tasks in linear production lines. SCARA robots, on the other hand, have a flexible, articulated design that allows for rapid, accurate torque application in various orientations, making them suitable for complex assembly processes. Both types improve efficiency and accuracy in manufacturing, contributing to the growing automation landscape.</p></p>
