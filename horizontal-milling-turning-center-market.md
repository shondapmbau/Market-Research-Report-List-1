<p><p>The horizontal milling-turning center market is categorized based on the axes of movement: 3-axis, 4-axis, and 5-axis machines. 3-axis centers operate along the X, Y, and Z axes, making them suitable for basic machining tasks. 4-axis centers add rotational movement on one axis, allowing for more complex shapes without repositioning the workpiece. 5-axis centers provide full flexibility with movement across five different axes, enabling intricate geometries and improved efficiency, particularly in aerospace and automotive applications.</p></p>
