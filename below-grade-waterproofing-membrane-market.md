The market features several types: </p><p>1. **Hard Mortar**: Rigid and durable, offering strong protection but less flexibility. </p><p>2. **Flexible Mortar**: Provides adaptability to substrate movement, enhancing longevity. </p><p>3. **Acrylic Ester**: Offers excellent adhesion and flexibility, suitable for varying climates. </p><p>4. **One Component Polyurethane**: Easy to apply, cures to a watertight finish, and is resistant to chemicals. Each type serves specific environmental and structural needs.</p></p>
