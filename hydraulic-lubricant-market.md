<p><p>The hydraulic lubricant market is segmented into **mineral lubricants** and **synthetic lubricants**. Mineral lubricants are derived from crude oil and are cost-effective, suitable for general-purpose applications with moderate operating conditions. Synthetic lubricants, on the other hand, are chemically engineered for superior performance, offering enhanced thermal stability, oxidation resistance, and efficiency in extreme temperatures or high-pressure environments. While mineral lubricants dominate due to affordability, synthetic lubricants are gaining traction in demanding industrial and automotive sectors for their durability and extended service life.</p></p>
