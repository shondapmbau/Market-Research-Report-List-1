<p><strong>The Online Ammonia Nitrogen Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ion-selective Electrodes (ISE)</li><li>Ultraviolet (UV) Absorbance</li><li>Colorimetric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The online ammonia nitrogen analyzer market comprises three primary types: Ion-selective Electrodes (ISE), Ultraviolet (UV) Absorbance, and Colorimetric methods. ISE utilizes specific electrodes to measure ammonia concentrations based on ion activity, offering real-time monitoring. UV Absorbance measures the light absorbance at specific wavelengths, providing accurate detection of ammonia levels. Colorimetric analysis involves chemical reactions that produce color changes corresponding to ammonia concentration, enabling visual assessment. Each method has unique advantages, catering to various industrial and environmental monitoring needs.</p></p>
