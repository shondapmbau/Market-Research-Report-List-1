Storage heating bidets maintain a reservoir of heated water, ensuring a constant supply for use but requiring more energy for heating the stored water. In contrast, instantaneous heating bidets heat water on demand, providing immediate warmth without storage, resulting in energy efficiency. Each type caters to different consumer preferences for comfort and efficiency in personal hygiene practices.</p></p>
