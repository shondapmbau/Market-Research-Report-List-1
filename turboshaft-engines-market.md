<p><strong>The Turboshaft Engines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial-Flow Type</li><li>Centrifugal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The turboshaft engine market is primarily categorized into axial-flow and centrifugal types. Axial-flow engines are characterized by their design, where air flows parallel to the axis of the engine, allowing for higher efficiency and power output, making them ideal for helicopters and large gas turbines. In contrast, centrifugal engines utilize a radial flow design, generating high pressure and suitable for smaller applications, such as auxiliary power units. Both types cater to diverse aerospace and industrial sectors, fueling growth in the market.</p></p>
