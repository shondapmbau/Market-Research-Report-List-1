<p><p>The 5G base station market comprises two primary types: macro and small cell markets. Macro base stations are large, high-power installations designed to cover extensive areas, typically deployed on towers or rooftops. They provide broad network coverage and handle substantial user traffic. In contrast, small cell base stations are compact, lower-power installations that enhance network capacity and coverage in densely populated urban environments. They complement macro stations by addressing local demand and enable improved performance in areas with high user density.</p></p>
