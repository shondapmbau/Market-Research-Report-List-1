<p><p>The oil-based printing inks market is divided into two main types: vegetable oil-based and mineral oil-based inks. Vegetable oil-based inks utilize renewable resources, such as soybean or linseed oils, offering environmental benefits and improved print quality. They are commonly used in eco-friendly applications. Conversely, mineral oil-based inks, derived from petroleum, are known for their durability and vibrant colors, making them popular for high-quality commercial printing. Both types cater to varying industry needs, balancing performance and sustainability.</p></p>
