<p><p>An articulated hauler is a type of heavy construction vehicle designed for transporting materials across rough terrains. It is characterized by its hinge between the cab and the cargo body, allowing for enhanced maneuverability and stability on challenging surfaces such as mud, gravel, or slopes. This vehicle is widely used in mining, construction, and forestry applications due to its ability to navigate tight spaces and carry heavy loads efficiently.</p><p>The Articulated Hauler Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.80% during the forecast period. Factors driving this growth include increasing infrastructure development globally, the rise in mining activities, and a growing emphasis on environmentally friendly construction solutions. Additionally, advancements in technology, such as automation and telematics, are being integrated into articulated haulers, enhancing their efficiency and reducing operational costs. </p><p>Market trends indicate a shift toward the adoption of electric and hybrid articulated haulers, responding to the demand for sustainable equipment. Furthermore, the recovery of construction and mining sectors post-pandemic is expected to bolster market demand, promoting continuous innovation and expansion in this segment.</p></p>
