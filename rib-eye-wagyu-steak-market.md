<p><p>Rib-eye Wagyu steak is categorized into four main market types based on breed and genetic characteristics. Kuroge Wagyu, or Black Wagyu, is renowned for its rich marbling and flavor, making it highly sought after. Akage Wagyu, or Red Wagyu, has leaner meat with a distinct taste but less intra-muscular fat. Mukaku Wagyu lacks horns, often produced for its mild flavor. Tankaku Wagyu is characterized by its unique feeding practices, influencing meat quality and yield, appealing to gourmet markets.</p></p>
