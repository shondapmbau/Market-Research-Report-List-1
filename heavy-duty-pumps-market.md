<p><p>The heavy-duty pumps market encompasses hydraulic and pneumatic pumps, essential for various industrial applications. Hydraulic pumps operate using pressurized fluids to transmit power, making them ideal for lifting heavy loads and facilitating precise control in machinery. In contrast, pneumatic pumps utilize compressed air to move fluids, offering benefits like lightweight design and portability, suitable for environments requiring quick and safe operation. Both types are crucial for efficient fluid management in construction, manufacturing, and other heavy industries.</p></p>
