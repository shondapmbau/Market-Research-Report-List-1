<p><p>The laser filter protection market comprises three primary types: </p><p>1. **Absorbing Glass Laser Filters** absorb harmful laser radiation, offering high optical density while ensuring minimal distortion, making them suitable for high-intensity applications.</p><p>2. **Plastic and Acrylic Laser Filters** are lightweight and impact-resistant, providing adequate protection while being versatile for various settings, though they may offer lower thermal resistance.</p><p>3. **Reflective Coated Laser Filters** reflect harmful laser wavelengths, minimizing transmission while maintaining high optical clarity, ideal for environments demanding precision and safety.</p></p>
