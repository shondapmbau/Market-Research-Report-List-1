<p><p>The Positron Emission Tomography (PET) scanner market is primarily segmented into full ring and partial ring PET scanners. Full ring PET scanners feature a complete 360-degree detection ring, providing enhanced image quality and uniformity, making them suitable for complex imaging. In contrast, partial ring PET scanners have incomplete detection rings and are often more compact and cost-effective, catering to smaller facilities or specialized applications. Both types serve critical roles in diagnostic imaging, depending on clinical needs and budgets.</p></p>
