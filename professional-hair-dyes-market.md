<p><strong>The Professional Hair Dyes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Temporary Hair Dye</li><li>Semi-permanent Hair Dye</li><li>Permanent Hair Dye</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The professional hair dye market is categorized into three main types: temporary, semi-permanent, and permanent hair dyes. Temporary hair dyes provide a short-lived color, typically washing out after one to several shampoos, suitable for experimental looks. Semi-permanent dyes last longer, generally fading over several weeks, offering a bridge between temporary and permanent options without the commitment. Permanent hair dyes, on the other hand, provide long-lasting results by chemically altering the hair structure, making them ideal for complete color transformations and gray coverage.</p></p>
