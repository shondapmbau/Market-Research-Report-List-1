<p><p>The adhesive-less Flexible Copper Clad Laminate (FCCL) market can be categorized into two main types: single-sided and double-sided. Single-sided FCCL features copper foil on one side, ideal for simpler applications where only one layer of circuitry is required. In contrast, double-sided FCCL has copper layers on both sides, allowing for more complex circuit designs and higher component density. These types cater to various industries, including electronics, automotive, and telecommunications, enhancing performance through reduced thickness and improved thermal properties.</p></p>
