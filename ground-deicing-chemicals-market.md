<p><p>The Ground Deicing Chemicals Market is categorized into two main types: liquid and solid. Liquid deicing agents, often composed of brine solutions or glycol-based products, rapidly penetrate ice and snow, providing quick thawing. They are commonly used in airports and highways for efficient surface treatment. Solid deicing chemicals, such as rock salt or calcium chloride, are applied in granular form and dissolve slowly, offering prolonged effectiveness. Both types are crucial for maintaining safety and accessibility during winter weather conditions.</p></p>
