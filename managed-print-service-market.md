<p><p>The Managed Print Service (MPS) market is categorized into three types: Cloud-based, On-premise, and Hybrid. Cloud-based MPS enables organizations to manage printing through online platforms, offering scalability and flexibility. On-premise MPS involves managing print infrastructure locally, providing control and security but often requiring higher upfront costs. Hybrid MPS combines both models, allowing firms to utilize cloud solutions while retaining critical print functions on-site. Each type addresses varying needs regarding budget, resources, and operational preferences.</p></p>
