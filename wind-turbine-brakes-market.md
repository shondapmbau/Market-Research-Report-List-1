<p><p>The wind turbine brakes market consists of two primary types: yaw brakes and rotor brakes. Yaw brakes are essential for controlling the turbine's orientation towards the wind, ensuring optimal energy capture and stability. They allow for precise adjustment of the rotor's angle. In contrast, rotor brakes are used to halt the spinning of the turbine blades during maintenance or in emergency situations, preventing damage and enhancing safety. Both brake types are crucial for maintaining efficient and secure wind turbine operations.</p></p>
