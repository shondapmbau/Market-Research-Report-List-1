<p><strong>The Portable Neutron Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas Proportional Detector</li><li>Scintillation Neutron Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable Neutron Detector market primarily consists of two types: Gas Proportional Detectors and Scintillation Neutron Detectors. Gas Proportional Detectors utilize gas ionization to measure neutron radiation by detecting secondary ionization resulting from neutron interactions. In contrast, Scintillation Neutron Detectors employ scintillation materials that emit light when neutrons interact with them, allowing for detection via photomultiplier tubes. Both technologies are essential for applications in nuclear safety, environmental monitoring, and medical diagnostics, offering distinct advantages in sensitivity and response time.</p></p>
