<p><ul><li>SMD Common Mode Inductor</li><li>Through Hole Common Mode Inductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The common mode inductor market is primarily divided into two types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) common mode inductors and through-hole common mode inductors. SMD common mode inductors are designed for compact applications, offering ease of integration on circuit boards with automated assembly processes. In contrast, through-hole common mode inductors are typically used in applications requiring greater power handling and durability. Both types are essential in noise filtering and electromagnetic interference suppression across various electronic devices and systems.</p></p>
