<p><ul><li>Axial Fans</li><li>Centrifugal Fans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The small size fans for cooling market primarily comprises axial and centrifugal fans. Axial fans move air parallel to the fan's axis, delivering high airflow at low pressure, making them ideal for ventilation in compact spaces. In contrast, centrifugal fans draw air into the center and expel it at right angles, generating higher pressure and airflow suitable for various applications, including HVAC systems and electronic cooling. Both types cater to diverse needs in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.</p></p>
