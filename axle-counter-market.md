<p><strong>The Axle Counter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rail Side Installation</li><li>On-rail Installation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The axle counter market is divided into two main types: rail side installation and on-rail installation. Rail side installation involves placing the axle counters alongside the tracks, allowing for monitoring and counting of train wheels from a safe distance. On-rail installation integrates the axle counters directly onto the railway tracks, enabling real-time detection and data collection while trains are in motion. Both types aim to enhance railway safety, improve operational efficiency, and ensure accurate train tracking.</p></p>
