<p><p>The tension sensors market includes various types designed for specific applications. The through shaft type offers direct measurement by passing through the shaft, ideal for situations where space is limited. The cantilever type, featuring a beam that flexes under load, measures tension through deflection, making it suitable for various industrial applications. Each type caters to different operational requirements and environments.</p></p>
