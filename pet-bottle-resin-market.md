<p><p>The PET bottle resin market includes **Conventional PET** and **Modified PET**. Conventional PET is widely used for packaging due to its clarity, strength, and recyclability, making it ideal for water, soda, and food containers. Modified PET, on the other hand, is engineered with additives or co-polymers to enhance properties like heat resistance, barrier protection, or durability, catering to specialized applications such as hot-fill beverages or UV-sensitive products. Both types address diverse packaging needs while maintaining sustainability and performance.</p></p>
