<p><ul><li>AMR</li><li>AMI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Gas Meter System market primarily consists of two types: Automated Meter Reading (AMR) and Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI). AMR enables one-way communication, allowing utility companies to collect meter data remotely, improving operational efficiency. In contrast, AMI offers two-way communication, enabling real-time data exchange between utilities and consumers, facilitating advanced analytics, billing accuracy, and enhanced customer engagement. Together, these technologies support better resource management and promote energy efficiency in the gas distribution sector.</p></p>
