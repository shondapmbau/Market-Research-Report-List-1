<p><strong>The Jacketed Vessels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Jackets</li><li>Half-Pipe Coil Jackets</li><li>Dimple Jackets</li><li>Plate Coils</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Jacketed vessels are crucial in various industrial processes for temperature control. Conventional jackets surround the vessel with a space for heating or cooling fluids. Half-pipe coil jackets feature a series of half-pipes wrapping the vessel, improving heat transfer efficiency. Dimple jackets consist of a series of dimples created on the jacket, providing effective heat exchange while minimizing material usage. Plate coils incorporate flat coils welded to the vessel, allowing for compact design and enhanced thermal performance, catering to diverse operational needs.</p></p>
