<p><strong>The Wheelchair Accessible Vehicle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Side Entry</li><li>Rear Entry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wheelchair accessible vehicle (WAV) market is primarily categorized into side entry and rear entry vehicles. Side entry WAVs feature a ramp that extends from the side, allowing easier access in tighter spaces and accommodating passengers and equipment comfortably. In contrast, rear entry models utilize a ramp that emerges from the back, providing a more traditional vehicle design. Each type serves specific needs, with side entry options often favored for passenger comfort, while rear entry vehicles are appreciated for their straightforward loading capabilities.</p></p>
