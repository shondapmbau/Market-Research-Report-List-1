<p><ul><li>Centralized</li><li>Modular</li><li>Distributed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Battery ECU market consists of three main types: centralized, modular, and distributed. Centralized systems concentrate battery management functions in a single unit, simplifying design but potentially limiting scalability. Modular systems use individual units that can be easily combined or replaced, enhancing flexibility and adaptability. Distributed systems spread management functions across multiple locations within the vehicle, optimizing performance and redundancy. Each type addresses different design priorities, influencing efficiency, cost, and ease of maintenance in electric vehicles.</p></p>
