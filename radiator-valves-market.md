<p><ul><li>Manual Radiator Valves</li><li>Thermostatic Radiator Valves</li><li>Traditional Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiator valves market comprises three main types: manual radiator valves, thermostatic radiator valves, and traditional valves. Manual radiator valves allow users to control the flow of water in radiators manually, offering straightforward functionality. Thermostatic radiator valves automatically regulate temperature by adjusting water flow based on room temperature, promoting energy efficiency. Traditional valves represent older, commonly used designs that lack advanced controls. Each type serves distinct needs in heating systems, influencing user comfort and energy consumption.</p></p>
