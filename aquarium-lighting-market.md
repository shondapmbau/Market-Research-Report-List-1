<p><p>The aquarium lighting market is primarily divided into two main types: traditional aquarium lighting and LED aquarium lighting. Traditional aquarium lighting typically includes fluorescent and incandescent bulbs, offering basic illumination but with higher energy consumption and shorter lifespans. In contrast, LED aquarium lighting provides energy-efficient, long-lasting alternatives, producing less heat and allowing for customizable light spectrums. LEDs enhance plant growth and coral health while reducing electricity costs, making them a popular choice among aquarists focused on sustainability and performance.</p></p>
