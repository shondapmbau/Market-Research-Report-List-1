<p><p>The single crystal growing furnace market is primarily segmented into several types based on the crystallization techniques used. The Czochralski Process (Cz) Furnace involves melting materials to pull out a single crystal, widely used for silicon and other semiconductors. The Float Zone (FZ) Furnace employs a zone melting technique to grow crystals without contaminants. The Bridgman Furnace utilizes a temperature gradient to facilitate crystal growth. Additionally, there are other specialized furnaces catering to unique materials and applications, expanding the market spectrum.</p></p>
