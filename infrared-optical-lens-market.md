<p><p>The Infrared Optical Lens market primarily includes aspheric and spherical lenses. Aspheric lenses feature a non-spherical surface that reduces optical aberrations, enhancing image quality and focusing capabilities, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Conversely, spherical lenses have consistent curvature and are simpler in design, often used in standard applications where precision is less critical. Both types cater to various industries, including telecommunications, surveillance, and medical imaging, driven by the demand for advanced infrared technologies and improved optical performance.</p></p>
