<p><p>Tray seeders are essential tools used in agricultural settings for efficient seed planting. The pneumatic needle type utilizes air pressure to precisely deliver seeds into trays, ensuring accurate spacing and depth. This method is beneficial for delicate seeds, minimizing damage. On the other hand, pneumatic drum seeders utilize a rotating drum mechanism to distribute seeds evenly across the planting trays. This type is more suited for larger quantities and a variety of seed sizes, enhancing planting efficiency and productivity.</p></p>
