<p><p>The Sterile Sampling Bottle market is categorized into two main types: those containing Sodium Thiosulfate and those without. Sterile Sampling Bottles with Sodium Thiosulfate are specifically designed to neutralize chlorine in water samples, making them ideal for microbiological testing in potable water. In contrast, Sterile Sampling Bottles without Sodium Thiosulfate are suited for samples where chlorine is not a concern, allowing for broad use in various laboratory analyses. Both types ensure contamination-free sampling for accurate results.</p></p>
