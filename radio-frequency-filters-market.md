<p><p>The Radio Frequency Filters market is primarily divided into three types: SAW (Surface Acoustic Wave) filters, BAW (Bulk Acoustic Wave) filters, and Others. SAW filters, known for their compact size and efficiency, are commonly used in mobile devices and communication systems. BAW filters, offering superior performance at higher frequencies, are favored in advanced telecommunications. The "Others" category encompasses various filtering technologies such as ceramic and LC filters, catering to niche applications in automotive, aerospace, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
