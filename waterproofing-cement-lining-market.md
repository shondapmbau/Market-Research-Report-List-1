<p><p>The waterproofing cement lining market is categorized into one-component and two-component systems. One-component systems consist of pre-mixed formulations that require no additional components for application, making them user-friendly and quick to apply. In contrast, two-component systems involve a separate hardener that is mixed with the base material, allowing for enhanced performance and durability. Both types are used in various applications, such as basements, water tanks, and infrastructure, providing essential protection against water infiltration and damage.</p></p>
