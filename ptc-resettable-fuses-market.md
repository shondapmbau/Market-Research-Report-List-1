<p><ul><li>CPTC</li><li>PPTC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PTC resettable fuses market is primarily categorized into two types: CPTC (Chip PTC) and PPTC (Polymeric PTC). CPTCs are compact, surface-mount devices ideal for high-density applications, providing overcurrent protection in small electronic circuits. PPTCs, on the other hand, are larger and often used in consumer electronics and industrial applications, offering reusability after a fault condition. Both types function by increasing resistance when a certain current threshold is exceeded, thereby protecting circuits from damage while allowing for automatic reset.</p></p>
