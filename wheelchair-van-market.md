<p><ul><li>Side Entry</li><li>Rear Entry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wheelchair van market includes two main types: side entry and rear entry. Side entry vans feature a ramp or lift on the side, allowing easy access for wheelchair users without requiring significant space behind the vehicle. Rear entry vans have a ramp or lift at the back, ideal for users who need more space for maneuvering. Both designs cater to accessibility needs, with side entry offering convenience in tight spaces and rear entry providing flexibility for larger wheelchairs or equipment.</p></p>
