<p><p>The vertical storage system market includes two primary types: Vertical Lift Modules (VLMs) and Vertical Carousels. VLMs consist of shelves that move vertically within a compact unit, allowing for high-density storage and easy retrieval of items, ideal for small parts. Vertical Carousels, on the other hand, utilize rotating shelves that move in a circular motion to bring stored items to the user, enhancing efficiency in picking and inventory management across various industries, such as manufacturing and healthcare.</p></p>
