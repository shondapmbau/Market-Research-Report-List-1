<p><p>The foodservice gloves market is categorized into three main types: latex gloves, rubber gloves, and non-latex gloves (nitrile or vinyl). Latex gloves offer flexibility and comfort but may cause allergic reactions. Rubber gloves, known for durability and resistance to chemicals, are ideal for heavy-duty tasks. Non-latex gloves, particularly nitrile and vinyl, provide excellent barrier protection and are preferred for allergy-sensitive users. Each type caters to specific needs in food safety, hygiene, and handling, ensuring safe food preparation and service.</p></p>
