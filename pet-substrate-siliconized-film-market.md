<p><p>The PET substrate siliconized film market is categorized into three primary types. Universal siliconized film is widely used for general applications, providing a non-stick surface suitable for a variety of products. Electrical insulating siliconized film is designed to offer high dielectric strength, making it ideal for electrical applications where insulation is critical. Laminating siliconized film is tailored for protective layering in packaging and industrial processes, ensuring durability and enhanced performance in laminated products. Each type serves distinct functions across various industries.</p></p>
