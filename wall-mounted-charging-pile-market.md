<p><p>The wall-mounted charging pile market is primarily categorized into AC charging piles and DC charging piles. AC charging piles convert alternating current from the electrical grid into a form suitable for electric vehicle (EV) charging. They are typically slower but more cost-effective. In contrast, DC charging piles deliver direct current directly to the EV, enabling faster charging times, making them ideal for high-demand environments. Both types cater to different user needs, balancing efficiency, speed, and installation considerations.</p></p>
